PHONE GRADING

RANK A

Grade A is the very best quality that you could reasonably expect from a refurbished MacBook or iPhone. Devices are in near-mint condition, they will be at an excellent cosmetic level and will show next to no signs of previous use.

RANK B

Grade B is the second best quality you can expect from a second hand device. While Grade B devices will have been tested thoroughly and are confirmed to be in working order, you may notice some light scuffing on the bevel or light scratches on the body.

RANK C

Grade C is the lowest quality of a used phone that is still in working order. Although second hand Grade C devices have been tested and are confirmed to work, they do show the signs of heavy or even excessive usage by their previous owner. They are however, cheaper than both grade A and Grade B phones
